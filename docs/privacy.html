Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 20, 2026

Little Chef ("we", "our", or "the app") is an offline-first meal planning application for Android. This policy explains what information we collect and how it's used.

1. Data We Collect

On-device data (not shared): All meal plans, recipes, pantry inventory, grocery lists, preferences (language, theme, font, serving size), and any API key you configure are stored locally on your device using Room database and DataStore. This data never leaves your device.

Firebase Analytics: We use Google Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage statistics (e.g., which screens are viewed, how often features are used). This helps us improve the app. No personally identifiable information is collected. You can opt out of analytics at any time in your device settings.

OpenAI API: If you choose to provide your own OpenAI API key for recipe scraping, the key is stored locally on your device. When you scrape a recipe URL, the URL is sent to OpenAI's API solely to process that request. We do not log or store the contents of your requests on our servers.

2. How We Use Data

  • To provide and maintain the app's core functionality (meal planning, pantry management, grocery lists)
  • To improve the app through anonymous usage analytics
  • To process recipe scraping requests when you provide an API key
  • To support in-app purchases via Google Play Billing (transaction data is handled by Google, not by us)

3. Third-Party Services

Firebase (Google): Anonymous analytics via firebase-analytics. Data is handled per Google's Privacy Policy. No advertising or tracking identifiers are used.

OpenAI: Only when you provide an API key and use the recipe scraping feature. Your requests are subject to OpenAI's privacy policy and API terms.

Google Play Billing: In-app purchases are processed by Google Play. We never receive or store payment information.

4. Data Retention

All on-device data remains on your device until you uninstall the app or clear app data. Firebase Analytics data is retained for 14 months. You can request deletion of analytics data by contacting us.

5. Your Rights

  • All your personal data is stored on your device — you have full control
  • Uninstall the app to remove all locally stored data
  • Opt out of analytics via device settings → Google → Analytics
  • Contact us to request analytics data deletion

6. Children's Privacy

Little Chef is designed for general family use and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

8. Contact

For questions about this policy, reach us at the Google Play Store listing for Little Chef.
